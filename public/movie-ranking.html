Best Movies To Watch On Netflix

Suspenseful horror films on Netflix

Run (2020 American film)

Run is a 2020 American psychological horror thriller film directed by Aneesh Chaganty and written by Chaganty and Sev Ohanian.

In the Tall Grass (film)

In the Tall Grass is a 2019 Canadian supernatural horror drama film written and directed by Vincenzo Natali.

Don't Breathe

Don't Breathe is a 2016 American horror-thriller film produced and directed by Fede Álvarez, co-produced by Sam Raimi and Robert Tapert, and co-written by Álvarez and Rodo Sayagues.
